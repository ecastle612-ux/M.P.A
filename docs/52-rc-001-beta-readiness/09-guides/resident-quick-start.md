# Resident Quick Start

**RC-001 · Design Partner beta**

## Sign in

Use the link from your property manager. Open **Tenant portal** → **Payments**.

## Pay rent

1. Review **Balance due** and upcoming charges.  
2. Add a card or ACH method (test mode may show sandbox methods).  
3. Tap **Pay balance**.  
4. Save your receipt from the Payments page.

## AutoPay

1. Add a payment method.  
2. Tap **Enroll** and accept the consent text.  
3. Disable anytime from the same screen.

## Messages & announcements

Use **Messages** and **Announcements** in the tenant portal. Preferences control notification channels (email/SMS may be limited in beta).

## If payment fails

You’ll see a plain-language error. Update your method and try again, or contact your property manager.
