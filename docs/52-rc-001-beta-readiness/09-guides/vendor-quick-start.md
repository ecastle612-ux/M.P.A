# Vendor Quick Start

**RC-001 · Design Partner beta**

## Important

The **Vendor portal is not active** in Design Partner beta. Property managers assign work and communicate from the M.P.A. manager app.

## What to expect

1. Your PM creates/assigns a work order to your vendor record.  
2. You may receive email/phone contact outside the portal (beta).  
3. Upload photos/docs via PM if they request media on the work order.  
4. Completion is recorded by the PM in M.P.A.

## Future

A full vendor portal (accept jobs, upload photos, message residents) is planned post-beta. Feedback on PM-mediated workflows is still valuable.
