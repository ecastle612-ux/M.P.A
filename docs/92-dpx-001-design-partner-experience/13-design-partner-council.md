**Package:** DPX-001  
**Amendment:** G  
**Status:** Planned — execute before public launch  
**Target:** 3–5 design partner companies

## Method

1. Recruit partners with real portfolios (not demo-only users).  
2. Give production (or production-like) access.  
3. **Do not teach** the product.  
4. Observe silently through Daily Operator Test scenarios ([11](./11-usability-amendments.md) Amendment B).  
5. Log every hesitation, question, look-without-click, and unexpected click → [05-friction-registry.md](./05-friction-registry.md).
