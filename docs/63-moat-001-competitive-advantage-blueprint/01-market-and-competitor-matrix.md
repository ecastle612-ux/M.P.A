## 2. Market analysis

### Buyer segments (relevant to M.P.A.)

| Segment | Primary pain | Who they use today | M.P.A. fit |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Owner-operator / small PM (≤100 units)** | Overwhelm, tool sprawl, learning tax | Avail, TurboTenant, DoorLoop, Buildium | High — ease + guided jobs |
| **Growing PM (100–1,000 units)** | Interrupt-driven ops, staff training, dual systems | AppFolio, Buildium, DoorLoop, Propertyware, Rentvine | **Core beachhead** |
| **Mid-market / complex accounting** | GL depth, owner reporting, controls | AppFolio, Yardi Breeze, Rent Manager, MRI | Selective — ops UX win first; accounting later |
| **Enterprise / institutional** | Scale, integrations, compliance theater | Yardi, MRI | Long-term; not Year-1 identity |

### Why switches happen (real reasons)

1. **Staff can’t run Monday without a trainer**  
2. **Maintenance + leasing + money live in different mental models**  
3. **Migration horror / dual entry for months**  
4. **Mobile triage fails between site walks**  
5. **AI/marketing promises don’t clear the queue**  
6. **Price + add-on fatigue** (secondary to daily pain)

### Competitive archetypes

| Archetype | Examples | Strength | Structural weakness |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Legacy density** | AppFolio, Propertyware, Rent Manager, MRI | Depth, brand, integrations | Training tax, admin UX |
| **Accounting-forward** | Buildium, Yardi Breeze | Books confidence | Ops/module hopping |
| **Modern SMB** | DoorLoop, Rentvine | Onboarding polish, UI | Still page-collection under chrome |
| **Landlord DIY** | Avail, TurboTenant | Cheap, simple listings/leases | Breaks at portfolio ops scale |

M.P.A. should **not** compete as “DIY landlord Lite” or “MRI clone.” Compete as **ops OS for growing PMs**.

### Reading the matrix

- **Win cells to force:** Daily workflow, AI ops-useful, Ease of learning, Communications, Migration, Automation-with-trust.  
- **Reach parity, don’t over-invest Year 1:** Accounting depth vs Buildium/MRI; Integrations vs AppFolio.  
- **Do not compete head-on Year 1:** MRI customization / enterprise reporting theater.
