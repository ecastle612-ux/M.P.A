# 04 — Tenant Experience

**Package:** DPX-003  
**Status:** Draft — awaiting Approve

---

## Principle

The tenant dashboard is **communication-first**.

Immediately after login, the resident must know:

1. Did management contact me?  
2. Do I owe rent?  
3. Is maintenance updated?  

Do **not** prioritize analytics over communication.

## Priority stack (IA)

### Priority 1 — Communications

| Order | Surface |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Announcements |
| 2 | Notifications |
| 3 | Messages |

### Priority 2 — Account operations

| Order | Surface |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | Rent |
| 2 | Maintenance |
| 3 | Lease |
| 4 | Documents |

## Requirements

- Above-fold: unread / urgent communications signals  
- Rent balance / next due visible without hunting  
- Open maintenance status glanceable  
- Analytics, charts, and secondary portfolio stats stay below fold or disclosed  

## Out of scope

New portal modules. Reorder and polish existing tenant portal surfaces only.
